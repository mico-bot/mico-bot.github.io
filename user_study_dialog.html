All User Study Dialog Transcripts

These are dialog transcriptions between the robot and human from the speech-to-speech interactions of ALL our user studies. We conducted 18 user studies with our robotic system in the real world, each with a unique participant. Participants were told to be as indifferent or as proactively helpful as they wished.
